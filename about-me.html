Hi there :-)

So, who is this girl?! My name is Mihaela and I am a student at Faculty of Automatic Control and Computer Science, developer, and traveller from Bucharest.

I am a computer science major, passionate about software infrastructure and data science. I'm interested in the latest technology in the industry and most of the time I like learning new things and solving problems.

I am doing computer science because I believe that computing is affecting the lives of everyone on the planet and I think is important to provide better tools for people to access the potential offered by computer. To me, programming is a way of looking at things.

I, Mihaela, started programming 4 years ago. I never stopped since. What made me start then was that I liked to visualize my ideas. Today I am keen on pursuing my BS in Computer Science and I use programming as a way to reason about phenomena and model them.


Some (other) things I enjoy:
At university and outside of university, I appreciate colors. Colors make me happy, all the variations, tints, hues, and gradients. Also composing them into Interfaces and the process of design and animation is energizing.
